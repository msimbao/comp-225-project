Welcome!

Thanks for Joining Tympdeja.

First, bookmark this page to quickly get to your feed.

Additionally, add an app shortcut to your smartphone:

  1. Launch “Chrome” app.
  2. Open Tympdeja and navigate to your home feed
  3. Tap the menu icon (3 dots in upper right-hand corner)
  4. Tap 'Add to homescreen.'

Select Your Teams

To start receiving news, first pick the teams you want to follow.

This is done in the 'Add Teams' section of the 'Teams' Page

To pick a team:

  1. Select A League that the team is under
  2. Select A Conference that the team is found in
  3. Tap the team you want to follow
  4. Press the back button to start over and pick other teams

Every team you've selected can be seen and removed in the 'My Teams' section

Once you're done go to your 'Feed' page to see the top 10 articles for each team
